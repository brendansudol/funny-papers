| **Our Dataset** | Text, Image, Video | Multilingual | ✓ | Yes |

Table 6: **Comparison of humor/meme benchmarks.** *Implicit* here denotes *harmful implicit jokes* (offense is non-obvious without understanding the joke). *Partial* indicates the dataset may contain such cases but they are not the main focus and/or not consistently annotated.
