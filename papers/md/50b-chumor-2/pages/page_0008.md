**Error Type II: Pun-based Error.** LLMs may fail to identify multiple meanings of a single word within a joke, causing them to fail on pun-based jokes where humor lies in inverting the conventional usage of words. Such jokes require LLMs to identify puns and the reason for the association of the multiple meanings. The overall process requires both scientific knowledge and creative thinking, which are challenging for LLMs.
