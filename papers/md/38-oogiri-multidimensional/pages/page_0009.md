## A. Prompt template

Figure 5 is the example of a prompt template for generation and evaluation. Please note that the template shown has been translated from the original Japanese into English for publication purposes.

[Figure: Prompt template box with two sections.]

**Prompt Template (generation)**

I will now present a prompt for an Oogiri. Please generate one funny answer according to the following constraints.  
# Constraints  
1. Generate a funny and humorous answer to the given Oogiri prompt.  
2. Do not repeat the prompt, and do not generate any content other than the answer to the Oogiri.  
(Image)  
prompt : {topic}

**Prompt Template (evaluation)**

Please evaluate the answer to the Oogiri topic according to the following evaluation criteria and output format.

Evaluation Criteria (For scores 0, 2, and 4, reference descriptions are provided. Please use these to assign a score.  
Scores 1 and 3 should be used for values in between.)  
Originality  
0: Not at all original (Something anyone would think of immediately)  
2: Somewhat original (Could be thought of with a little effort)  
4: Highly original (A unique perspective that others are unlikely to conceive)

Clarity  
0: Completely incomprehensible (The meaning is unclear even after multiple readings)  
2: Somewhat understandable  
4: Easily understandable (The meaning is immediately clear)

Relevance  
0: Completely irrelevant to the topic  
2: Somewhat relevant to the topic  
4: Highly relevant to the topic

Intellect  
0: Not intellectual (Silly or absurd)  
2: Somewhat intellectual  
4: Highly intellectual (Shows intelligence or erudition)

Relatability  
0: Not at all relatable (Doesn't resonate; the situation or feeling is unimaginable)  
2: Somewhat relatable  
4: Highly relatable (The situation or feeling is very easy to understand and empathize with)

Overall Funniness  
0: Not funny at all  
2: Somewhat funny  
4: Very funny

Output Format  
You must respond in the following format and nothing else.  
{  
"Originality": [integer from 0-4],  
"Clarity": [integer from 0-4],  
"Relevance": [integer from 0-4],  
"Intellect": [integer from 0-4],  
"Relatability": [integer from 0-4],  
"Overall_Funniness": [integer from 0-4]  
}  
(Image)  
Topic: {topic} Answer: {answer}
