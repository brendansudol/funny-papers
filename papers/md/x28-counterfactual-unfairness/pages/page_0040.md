In this validation, we used the *complex* condition, where each speaker is represented through a full intersectional profile across ten identity dimensions.

As shown in Table 15, both GPT-4o and Llama-3-70B produce the same directional asymmetry across all three evaluation criteria. Humor produced by privileged speakers targeting marginalized listeners receives lower acceptance scores, higher social sensitivity ratings, and higher character consistency scores from both evaluators.
