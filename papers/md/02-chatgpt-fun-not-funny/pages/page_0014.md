**D.2 Modification B - Minus Topic**

To create the samples of this condition, the joke-typical and potentially humorous topic was removed from the jokes. For instance, in the case of personification, i.e., if the protagonist of the original joke was an object, such as a tomato or a computer, it was replaced by a neutral, human protagonist, such as a teacher or a scientist. The typical question-answer format remained unchanged. However, just as in Modification A, the actual pun gets lost.
