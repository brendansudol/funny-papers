**Adversarial multimodal-pun tests:** [**“I See What You Did There”: Can Large Vision-Language Models Understand Multimodal Puns?**](https://aclanthology.org/2026.acl-long.444/) (Xu, Sheng, Li et al., ACL 2026) `peer-reviewed` `dataset` `benchmark` `method` introduces **MultiPun**, with 445 homophonic or homographic image-text puns and 890 substitution-based non-pun distractors. Detection, pun-component localization, and explanation tests show that most VLMs over-associate playful visual scenes and familiar phrasing with puns. Pun-CoT and Pun-Tuning improve F1 scores by 16.5% on average, but the adversarial negatives are the more important contribution: they test whether a model can reject a near-match rather than merely narrate a plausible joke.
